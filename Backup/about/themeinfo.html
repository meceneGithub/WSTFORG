2013 Theme - Purpose and Destiny

The word of God says that we are pilgrims on this earth, aliens, ambassadors, and people in this world but not of this world. As Christian women on a journey, do you know what you are supposed to do here on earth until you get to heaven?

The people of Israel found themselves wondering in Babylon in exile and asking the same question, what now? God told them that he would set them free from exile after 70 years, and they were asking God what they were to do while waiting in Babylon. As Christian women, we have been saved, sanctified and waiting to go to heaven to meet our Lord and Savior, have you ever find yourself asking the same question as the people of Israel, what am I suppose to do here on earth while I wait to get to heaven?

In Jeremiah 29, God gave the people of Israel specific instructions and those principles still apply to us today as pilgrims on earth. On October 11-12-2013, Women Seeking the Father Ministry would like to invite Haitian Women nationwide to gather in Chicago for another exciting, Spirit-filled, life changing weekend learning on principles for becoming a woman of purpose and destiny out of the theme verse of Jeremiah 29:11-12

"For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord, “plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future. Then you will call upon me and come and pray to me, and I will listen to you.(NIV)"

We have a great line up of women speakers that God has anointed to bring this timely message to Haitian women during such a time of uncertainly. We are looking forward to see the women who have attended past retreats and praying for more women to catch the vision of this ministry; a ministry dedicated to encourage Haitian to get to know God deeper, to love Him dearly and to draw nearer to his heart.

Come with a thirty heart and drink from the fountain of life living water!
