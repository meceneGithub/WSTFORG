Directions to Pheasant Run Resort


From South Side Chicago

  • Take I- 94 west to I-90 West to Route 59 South.
  • Exit south on Route 59 and continue to North Avenue (Route 64).
  • Turn right (west) on North Avenue.
  • Pheasant Run IL is located 3 miles west of Route 59 on North Avenue (Route 64) on the south side of the street.

From North Side Chicago

  • Take I-90 West to Route 59 South.
  • Exit south on Route 59 and continue to North Avenue (Route 64).
  • Turn right (west) on North Avenue.
  • Pheasant Run IL is located 3 miles west of Route 59 on North Avenue (Route 64) on the south side of the street.


or LOCAL ROUTE
  • Take Golf ave West to Route 59 continue to North Ave (Route 64)
  • make Right on North ave (Route 64) continue to the resort
  • Pheasant Run IL is located 3 miles west of Route 59 on North Avenue (Route 64) on the south side of the street.

From Chicago Midway

  • Take Cicero Avenue North to I-55 South.
  • Get on I-55 South and continue to I-355 North.
  • Exit onto I-355 North and continue to I-88 West.
  • Exit onto I-88 West and continue to Farnsworth (Kirk) North.
  • Exit north on Farnsworth (Kirk) and continue to North Avenue (Route 64).
  • Turn right (east) on North Avenue and continue approximately 1 mile.
  • The Resort is located on the south side of the street.

From the South

  • Take I-294 North to I-88 West.
  • Exit I-88 West at Farnsworth (Kirk Rd.) North and continue to North Avenue (Route 64).
  • Turn right (east) on North Avenue and continue approximately 1 mile.
  • The Resort is located on the south side of the street.
or
  • Take I-55 North to I-355 North to I-88 West.
  • Exit I-88 West at Farnsworth (Kirk Rd.) North and continue to North Avenue (Route 64).
  • Turn right (east) on North Avenue and continue approximately 1 mile.
  • The Resort is located on the south side of the street.
