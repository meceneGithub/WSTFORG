Retreat Details and Information

The 2015 10th Annual Hatian Women Retreat will be held at the beautiful Pheasant Run Resort St Charles, Illinois.
The objective of this year’s retreat is a call for women of God to know that Jesus Christ is the same yesterday and today and forever. With a line of great speakers, we are expecting great things from God and for you to have an encounter with God. Registration for this year retreat is opened from June 1, 2015 - August 31, 2015.

You can Click Here to Register on the web site or contact your church representative or you can call us at (847) 901-3016 for more information.
