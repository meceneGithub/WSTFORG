Speakers Bio

Why are you here? and what is your Destiny

Most people define faith as trusting God, faith is confidence of things hope for or faith is to believe. Faith, the very heart of the gospel, to believe what someone says. Faith involves believing that something is true. I am here to invite you to our annual Haitian women retreat that takes place every year in the heart of the Chicago land area.

The objective of this year’s retreat is a call for women of God to learn to walk by faith and not by sight. It is also a call to believe in the Word of God; to abandon all merely human security, and thus to overcome the despair which arises from the attempt to find security outside the will of God which is always vain. Abandoning the attempt to build security through our won efforts and placing our trust in God. We restrict trust to what we can see. Faith means abandoning the quest for tangible realities and transitory objects. The pursuit of seen things is sin, for by it we exclude the invisible reality from our lives and refuse God’s future, which is offer as a gift. Faith also involves obedience, turning our backs on self and abandoning all security.
