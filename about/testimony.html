Testimonies

Last years conference was explosive!!!!
I really had no expectations; however after two days of hearing the word which was lead by powerful women of God, the praise and worship, and the fellowship I shared with the other participants this is a conference I look forward to attending again this year.
--Sindy Mondesir--

I can't begin to explain how I was blessed by everything that took place at the retreat last year. I went for the first time last year thinking I was doing Emma a favor by supporting the ministry. However, I didn’t realize that I went because God wanted me there and that He had stored something special for me. I enjoyed the fellowship with all of the women.
The worship time with Mecene, the band and the singers was awesome! The hotel was stunning and the lunch and dinner that was provided were delicious. The topic was very moving and so true to so many women’s lives. God’s presence was most definitely there as I saw the Him work through those women, including myself.
I am touched and encouraged by Emma’s love for God. I give God all the praise and glory for working through Emma to put together such a well organized, Spirit filled retreat. Last year was a beginning of something great for me and I can’t wait to see what God has in store for me this year."
--Lodze Noel--

The retreat was great. It provided me an opportunity to get away from all the noise and be alone with God. The friendship, the fellowship with other Haitian sisters in Christ was something you can't really put a price on. I appreciate the chance to be with Haitian women from different states and share my testimonies and prayer time together.
God is good.
