What is the Goal of the Women Seeking the Father Ministry?

  • The goal of this ministry is to help Haitian women of all ages
  • To live a life of abundance, for Jesus said, "I have come that they may have life and that they may have it more abundantly" (John 10:10)
  • Discover their true identity in Christ who redeemed them
  • to empower them to live a life of victory over adversities
  • To live a life of freedom from the captivity of past failures and defeats
  • To embrace and accept the pure, unconditional love of God the Father
  • To become women of destiny and purpose
